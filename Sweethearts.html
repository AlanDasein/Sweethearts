SWEETHEARTS is a very easy game to play.

FIRST select an amount to bet and how many bets to do.
SECOND select with how many lives you would like to play.
THIRD Hit the button DEAL to start the game.
FOURTH click any uncovered card from the deck to reveal it.
NOTICE The selectors and the DEAL button are located at the bottom bar.
Right next to the deck of cards you can see the bet you placed, how much you won, as well as the remaining lives and the hearts collected in the current game.
The payouts table will show how much can be won according with the hearts collected, the bet placed and the number of lives being played.
The game will finish as soon as all the lives are being consumed and its objective is to reveal as many hearts as you can.

GAMEFLOW

EVERYTIME you hit an uncovered card from the deck you will consume one life
EVERYTIME you uncover a card with a heart you will collect one heart
HOWEVER there are multiple bonus cards that can change this dynamic for better or worse

There are 80 cards on the deck, 20 HEART CARDS, 15 NEUTRAL CARDS and 6 types of bonus cards.

 10 EXTRA LIFE CARDS
If you hit this card you will get an extra life
10 MINUS LIFE CARDS
If you hit this card you will lose an extra life
10 MINUS HEART CARDS
If you hit this card you will lose one of the hearts you have
5 GRENADE CARDS
2 cards around the card you are clicking will be uncovered
5 HINT CARDS
3 cards from the deck will be highlighted and one of them will be a heart
5 GAME OVER CARDS
The game will stop as soon as you hit this card
